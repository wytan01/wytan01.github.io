Welcome!

Hi there, I'm Wei Yi! I recently graduated from Purdue University with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Psychology. With my major in Computer Science, I chose to pursue a concentration in machine intelligence and security because I am always curious as to how AI works (which isn't as complicated once you break it down!) and I love a good challenge. In fact, one of my favorite classes I have taken for the security track involved lots of "hacking"!

Some of the clubs I had joined in my time at Purdue were the Computer Science Undergraduate Student Board (USB), Women In Science Program (WISP), and BoilerMake. These clubs allow me to give back to the CS/DS community or provide the same enjoyable experience I had at their events!

Given the chance, I enjoy seeking new experiences, whether that be attending various concerts or skydiving 13,500 feet at Indianapolis! Besides my technical background, I am also just a normal girl in her 20s, trying to figure out how to do this "adulting" thing while having as much fun as I can!
